About ANP & Sunshine.

ANP Privado is a project by ANP in association with Sunshine Developers — names already woven into Baner's western corridor through addresses such as ANP One on Balewadi High Street and ANP THINQ. Privado continues that intent: low-density, design-led living where space, scale and refinement meet seamlessly.

Conceived as an abode of privacy, every decision here — from the ~200 ft tower-to-tower spacing to the 80% open space and the layered natural landscape by Singapore-based architects — is engineered around a single idea: a home that echoes your own pace, untouched by the world outside.

Questions

Answered.

When is ANP Privado launching?+
Show flats are ready and bookings are open. Pre-registered buyers receive priority allocation, launch-phase pricing, and first access to the most-favoured towers and corner residences before the wider release.
Where is the project located?+
ANP Privado, Sr. No. 16, Pan Card Club Road, Baner, Pune 411045 — Pune's western corridor. Approximately 5 minutes from Balewadi IT Park and Manipal Hospital, 7 minutes from Balewadi High Street, and well connected to the Mumbai–Bangalore Highway.
What residence types are available?+
All-corner 3 & 4 BHK residences. 3 BHK carpet areas of 1309, 1489 and 1276 sqft; 4 BHK carpet areas of 1855, 2306 and 2228 sqft; plus a signature 4 BHK of 3000 sqft carpet (4200 sqft saleable) in Tower 1. Every bedroom has an en-suite washroom, with a powder room in every home.
What amenities does the project offer?+
65+ curated amenities across a 50,000 sq.ft grand clubhouse and a layered podium landscape — an all-season temperature-controlled pool, a 105 m lazy river, vitality pool, private luxe theatre, regal banquet, champion's arena (badminton, squash, cricket simulator), elite fitness studio, Miyawaki greenwoods, canopy walks and a private skydeck.
What are the specifications?+
A-class earthquake-resistant RCC with Aluform technology, 3400 mm floor-to-floor height, hand-picked imported marble flooring, Geberit concealed plumbing, Hansgrohe CP fittings, Duravit ceramic ware, centralised heat-pump hot water, VRV provision, IoT power monitoring and a MyGate-based security ecosystem with video door phone and CCTV.
Who is the developer?+
ANP Privado is a project by ANP in association with Sunshine Developers, an established name in Baner's western corridor with addresses including ANP One and ANP THINQ. Landscaping is led by Singapore-based architects.
What is the project composition?+
A 10-acre, low-density township of 9 towers (Towers 1–8 at G+33 and Tower 9 at G+34), with 80% open space, a ~200 ft tower-to-tower gap and all-corner apartments framed by Pashan Hill.
How do I book a site visit?+
Site visits are by appointment. Message us on WhatsApp at +91 88570 90799 and our team will arrange a private visit to the ready show flat at your preferred time.
Is ANP Privado a good investment?+
Baner is among West Pune's most established residential micro-markets, with strong rental demand and proximity to Balewadi IT Park, Hinjewadi and the Mumbai–Bangalore Highway. Low-density, all-corner inventory on a 10-acre parcel is rare in this corridor, and early entry offers the strongest point in the project's pricing lifecycle.
